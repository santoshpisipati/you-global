Option 1 price
(upfront) *
This is the upfront once only fee you'd like us to offer the Client, and is usually a percentage of between 10 and 30% of annualised remuneration, however other formulas can be considered, eg. flat fees.

Option 2 price
(eg. quarterly) *
We provide most Clients a quarterly payment option, which effectively includes a pro rata guarantee (Candidate leaves, Clients stops paying).

There is greater risk and slower cash flow for us with this option, therefore the total of 4 quarterly payments should be 20 to 30% higher than a single upfront payment. Example: 15% upfront, or 4.5% or 5% per quarter for 4 quarters.

Type N/A if you do not want to complete this field, or if the Cient is not to be offered a pricing / guarantee option, but keep in mind that options = value.

If uncertain, please see WIKI > MAIN PAGE > PRICING & NEGOTIABLE TERMS.
Option 3 price
(eg. monthly) *
You may want to provide a Client with a monthly payment option (instead of or in addition to quarterly), which effectively includes a pro rata guarantee (Candidate leaves, Clients stops paying).

There is greater risk and slower cash flow for us with this option, therefore the total of 12 monthly payments should be higher than a single upfront payment, and higher than quarterly payments. Example: 12.5% upfront, or 3.75% per quarter for 4 quarters, or 1.5% per month for 12 months.

Type N/A if you do not want to complete this field, or if the Cient is not to be offered a 3rd option, but keep in mind that options = value.

If uncertain, please see WIKI > MAIN PAGE > PRICING & NEGOTIABLE TERMS.
